Brazilian Carrot Cake

Description:

Simple and easy to do this recipe is a people pleaser. This cake is very popular in Brazil, especially at breakfast and as a snack during the day. With a irresistable chocolate syrup and a moist and fluffy interior, it's impossible to resist!

Check out below the step by step to create this delicious cake

INGREDIENTS (8 SERVINGS):

    Cake Batter
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups of wheat flour
  • 3 medium grated carrots
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 soup spoon of baking powder
    Chocolate Syrup
  • 1 soup spoon of butter
  • 3 soup spoon cocoa powder Eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of sugar
    Steps
  1. In a blender, add the carrots, eggs and oil, then mix it.
  2. Add the sugar and blend again for 5 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, add the flower and mix it until the lumps are gone.
  4. Add the baking powder and mix it slowy with a spoon.
  5. Preheat the oven and bake the batter at 180ÂºC for approximately 40 minutes.
  6. As the cake bakes, pick up a bowl and put all the ingredients of the chocolate syrup and blend it throughly.
  7. Bring the result to the stove and keep blending until you reach a creamy consistency.
  8. After the cake and the syrup are done, dump the syrup on top of the cake and enjoy!!
